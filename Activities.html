BIRD WATCHING WITH EXPERTS

Varieties of birds comprises of, beautiful parrot species, flightless birds, aquatic and semi aquatic birds and many others. The zoo's entrance is adorned with a group of flamingos. The fastest and largest flightless bird, the ostrich, is housed in a nearby spacious enclosure. Walk-in aviaries are ; endemic and indigenous birds and exotic birds. Within beautifully landscaped aviaries, visitors can observe free-flying birds in a shady and cool setting. Learn all about birds from our experts who are great guides that will give you all the information you need to have a wonderful experience. Learn more: Bird Watching with the Experts

MEET THE ELEPHNATS

The Elephant Presentation showcases how majestic these mammals are. Besides sharing interesting facts and knowledge, our elephant keepers and presenters, will allow you to in on the unique quirks and personalities of our beautiful native elephants. No visit to Sri Lanka will be complete without this experience of watching them. Explore, and interact with our keepers. You may just be one of the guests that gets to feed them, do pay a visit to their show. The elephant presentation promises to be an instructional and facinating experience, one that aims to foster and convey conservation awareness. Learn more: Meet the Elephants.

BOTANICAL GARDEN TOUR

You can visit the zoo which includes a lovely botanical garden which is free to visit. It is a lucious landscape that will surely take your breath away. It's beauty will draw you in and give you a peace of mind. It is definetly a must-see when you are visiting the zoo so be sures to visit our plantation team to get advice about plants as well; as we are fully committed to the ever-green-life and zero waste recycling conecpt. There are tour guides available for the tours at the botanical gardens between 9:00 am - 2:00 pm.
