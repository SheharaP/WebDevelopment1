ABOUT SRI LANKA

The Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is a beautiful tropical island in South of Asia, located in the Indian Ocean is also known as "the pearl of the Indian Ocean". Sri Lanka is diverse with all kinds of rich cultural heritages the country has inherited from a very prolonged history.
It is also home to many wild animals who have resided within wilderness rain/forests and animal sanctuaries that are in high demand for tourist attraction. Sri Lanka has unique cultural and geographical features that include remarkable combination of captivating landscapes, pristine beaches and astounding historical sites. ABOUT NATIONAL ZOOLOGICAL GARDEN

At the heart of the country's Capitol, Colombo Mount-Lavinia, Dehiwala the National Zoological and Botanical Garden which was founded in 1936 is still home to many species of animals. The zoo exhibits animals but also places an emphasis on animal conservation and welfare, and education on perserving animal species. The Museum of National Zoological Gardens of Sri Lanka exhibits remains such as skeletons, footprints, eggs, excrements and specimen as well as preserved mammals and birds.
We have accomplished, hard working, kind and friendly animal lovers who tend the wellbeing of our animals. The aquarium, walk through aviary, reptile house and butterfly garden is home to 2500-3000 species that we take care of. Our staff is most delighted to assist our beloved visitors with educational and entertainment tours around the zoo.
Dehiwala Zoological Garden is one of the oldest zoological gardens in Asia and is very diverse in species. Such as;

  • Mammals
  • Reptiles
  • Birds
  • Fish

OUR ANIMALS AND PLANTS

The zoo consists of diverse indigenous animals from many parts of the world. The zoo has well-landscaped gardens especially enough to accomadate to our animals.
To provide more natural habitats some lawns of the Zoo have been converted into small forest patches which are rich in rare plants. There are a valuable number of plants such as rare medicinal plants including herbs, shrubs, climbers and creepers. Also our lovely botanical garden is a welcoming peaceful sanctuary for everyone.
